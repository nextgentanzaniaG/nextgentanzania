Escrow Account Services

Secure, neutral holding of transaction funds — protecting both buyer and seller until all conditions are met.

What is an Escrow Account?

An escrow account is a neutral third-party account where transaction funds are held until all agreed conditions (title verification, legal clearances, property handover) are fully satisfied. Neither buyer nor seller can access the funds until both parties' obligations are met — protecting everyone in the transaction.

🏦
Property Purchase Escrow
Deposit and balance payments held securely. Funds released only upon verified title transfer and property handover. Prevents fraud and title disputes.
🔨
Auction Escrow
Bid deposits and auction proceeds held in escrow throughout the bidding and documentation process. Bank-compliant escrow for all auction transactions.
🌍
International Transfer Escrow
For foreign buyers sending funds from overseas — funds held in escrow while TIC/ZanInvest registration and title verification are completed.
🏗️
Developer / Off-Plan Escrow
Stage payments for off-plan developments held in escrow and released to developer only upon completion of agreed construction milestones.

How Escrow Works — Step by Step

1
Open Escrow Account
Buyer and seller agree to use escrow. NEXT-GEN opens a dedicated escrow account at a partner bank. Terms of release are agreed and documented.
2
Deposit Funds
Buyer deposits agreed amount (deposit or full purchase price). Funds are held — neither party can withdraw. Buyer receives acknowledgement of receipt.
3
Conditions Verified
NEXT-GEN verifies all agreed conditions: title deed clean, property as described, all documentation signed and in order.
4
Release of Funds
Upon verification of all conditions, funds are released to seller. Property keys and title deed handed to buyer simultaneously. Transaction complete.
✅ Why Use Escrow?

Tanzania's property market carries real risks of fraud — fake title deeds, double-selling, and non-delivery after payment. Escrow eliminates these risks. We strongly recommend escrow for all transactions above TZS 50 million.
