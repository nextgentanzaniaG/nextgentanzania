🏛️ Regulatory Bodies & Licensing

📋
BRELA
Business Registrations and Licensing Agency. NEXT-GEN Holdings Company Limited is fully registered under BRELA. All transactions backed by registered company status.
⚖️
Licensed Auctioneers
Fully licensed to conduct property auctioneering including court, bank, government and estate auctions under Tanzania's Auctioneers Act.
💰
TRA — Tanzania Revenue Authority
Full compliance with Tanzania Revenue Authority. All transactions handled with proper TIN documentation and stamp duty compliance.
🌍
TIC — Tanzania Investment Centre
We facilitate TIC registration for mainland Tanzania property purchases by foreign investors. Authorised to assist with the full application process.
🏝️
ZanInvest / ZIPA
Zanzibar Investment Promotion Authority. We assist foreign investors with ZIPA registration for Zanzibar property purchases — no minimum investment.
🗺️
Ministry of Lands
We conduct all title deed searches, surveys, and transfer registrations at the Ministry of Lands and Human Settlements Development.
