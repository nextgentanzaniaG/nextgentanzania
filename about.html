Our Journey

From Startup to Tanzania's Premier Platform

What began as a bold vision to modernise Tanzania's property market has grown into the country's most technology-forward real estate and auctioneering company.

🌱
2022
Founded — Kilimo Dili launched as a sole proprietorship in Dar es Salaam, initially focusing on agricultural land and rural property transactions.
🏢
2023
Expansion — Moved into urban real estate, bank auction facilitation, and debt collection services. First partnerships with CRDB and NMB Bank established.
🚀
2024
Technology Launch — Launched nextgentanzania.com, Tanzania's first AI-powered property platform. Expanded to Zanzibar and all 31 regions.
🏛️
2025
Incorporation — Formally incorporated as NEXT-GEN HOLDINGS COMPANY LIMITED under BRELA. Licensed auctioneer status confirmed.
🌍
2026
Going Global — International investor network expanded to Europe, Middle East, and Asia. NextGen AI launched in 5 languages. 500+ properties transacted.
